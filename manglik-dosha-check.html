What is Manglik Dosha?

Manglik Dosha occurs when Mars (Mangal) is placed in specific houses in the birth chart. It is considered important in Vedic astrology for marriage compatibility.

A person is considered Manglik if Mars is in 1st, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house.
