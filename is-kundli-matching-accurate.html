Is Kundli Matching Accurate?

Let’s understand the truth behind Kundli matching and astrology compatibility.

What is Kundli Matching?

Kundli matching is a traditional Vedic astrology method used to compare the birth charts of two individuals for marriage compatibility. It is based on the Gun Milan system (36 points).

Is it 100% accurate?

Kundli matching is not scientifically proven to predict exact life outcomes. However, it is widely used as a traditional guideline in many cultures.

What it actually shows

  • Personality compatibility tendencies
  • Emotional and behavioral patterns
  • Marriage compatibility suggestions

What it does NOT guarantee

  • Exact future prediction
  • Guaranteed successful marriage
  • Fixed life outcomes

Conclusion

Kundli matching should be seen as a traditional belief system that helps in guidance, not as a fixed prediction of life events.
