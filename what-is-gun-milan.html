What is Gun Milan?

Gun Milan is a 36-point system used in Vedic astrology for marriage compatibility.

Gun Milan is an ancient Vedic astrology method used to match horoscopes of bride and groom. It checks compatibility based on 8 key factors like Varna, Vashya, Tara, Yoni, Graha Maitri, Gana, Bhakoot and Nadi.

A higher score means better compatibility. Generally, 18+ out of 36 is considered acceptable.
