What is Gun Milan?

Gun Milan is a Vedic astrology system used to evaluate marriage compatibility. It compares 8 key factors like Varna, Vashya, Tara, Yoni, Graha Maitri, Gana, Bhakoot and Nadi.

The maximum score is 36 points. Higher score means better compatibility for marriage.
